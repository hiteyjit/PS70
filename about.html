About Me

Hello! I'm Hiteyjit, a sophmore at Harvard passionate about the intersection of technology and creativity. Through PS70, I've been exploring the fascinating world of digital fabrication and engineering design.

I am a student on exchange from the University of Toronto, currently specializing in Neuroscience. I have had a background in robotics since childhood but recently pivoted towards Neuroscience during my undergraduate studies. My aim for joining Harvard's VUS program is to deepen my knowledge in certain areas of robotics, neuroscience, and their combination, which are explored quite in-depth at Harvard.

I am currently investigating neuronal measurment techniques using some principles of engineering, and am excited for what's to come!

In my free time, I like to play Tennis, sleep (I love sleep), watch Forumla 1, go Go Karting, fly my drone (which I recently crashed :(( ), and make films!
